Our Story

Kapitan Pakpak started as a simple idea: serve wings that feel “sulit” — crispy, flavorful, and perfect for sharing. From family cravings to barkada hangouts, we built a place where everyone can eat well without breaking the budget.

We keep it simple: good ingredients, bold sauces, fast service, and a vibe that feels like home. Come hungry — leave happy.
